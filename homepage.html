GOAL 4- Quality Education

By Samaira and Ayaan

Goal 4 of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) is about making sure that everyone has access to a good education that helps them learn important skills and knowledge. This includes making sure that education is fair and equal for everyone, and that people can learn throughout their whole lives. The goal also wants to help people who may not have had access to education before, like girls and people from poorer communities, to have the opportunity to learn and succeed.
